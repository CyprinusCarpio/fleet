Detailed Description

A extended alternative to Fl_Tile.

TileEx is used to maintain a tesselated layout of widgets. The child widgets must fill the available space entirely, and not overlap. TileEx keeps a record of minimal, preferred and maximal sizes for all child widgets. Widgets cannot go below the minimal size. Widgets will attempt to scale up to their preferred sizes during resizing of the TileEx, but can go above it when it's required for maintaining tesselation. Widgets will not go over their maximal sizes. Dragging on the edge between widgets updates their preferred sizes.
